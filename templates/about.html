About Electrical Edge

Where Electrical Engineers Learn and Grow

Our Mission

Electrical Edge is an educational platform created to help Electrical Engineering students access high-quality notes, eBooks, formula sheets, previous year questions, and exam-focused study resources in one place.

Our goal is to make Electrical Engineering learning more structured, affordable, and practical for B.Tech students, diploma students, and competitive exam aspirants.
